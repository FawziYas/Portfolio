Vision

Building the Future of Healthcare

My work sits deliberately at the intersection of two fields that are converging faster than most realize: clinical medicine and intelligent systems.

Medicine Artificial Intelligence Neurosurgery Microsurgery Robotic Surgery Digital Health Medical Education

I am drawn to the operating room as much as I am to the codebase — to neurosurgery and microsurgery as disciplines of extraordinary precision, and to robotic and AI-assisted surgical systems as the next evolution of that precision. At the same time, I see medical education as the multiplier: every OSCE platform, every AI-assisted learning tool, every clinical assessment system I build is ultimately about training better clinicians, faster and more consistently.

My long-term vision is to contribute meaningfully to the future of AI-powered surgery, intelligent healthcare systems, and next-generation medical education technology — where clinical expertise and engineering discipline move forward together, not in parallel.
