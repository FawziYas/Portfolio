About

Medicine, engineered with intent

I am a Medical Doctor and Research & Teaching Assistant with a passion for combining medicine, education, and technology to improve healthcare. Alongside my clinical and academic work, I specialize in designing intelligent medical software that enhances education, assessment, and patient care.

My interests extend beyond traditional medicine into Artificial Intelligence, Digital Health, Robotics, and Medical Informatics. I believe the future of healthcare lies in the seamless integration of AI with clinical practice, particularly in neurosurgery, microsurgery, and robot-assisted surgical systems.

I have led and contributed to multiple projects involving medical education platforms, clinical assessment systems, hospital management software, and AI-assisted learning tools. I enjoy solving complex healthcare problems through software engineering while maintaining a strong commitment to patient care and medical education.

My long-term vision is to contribute to the future of AI-powered surgery, intelligent healthcare systems, and next-generation medical education technologies.
